About me

I have completed my PhD in Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, under the supervision of Prof. Partha Sarathi Mandal. I am currently a visiting scientist at Indian Statistical Institute Kolkata and working with Dr. Anisur Rahaman Molla. My research interests include Distributed algorithms, Algorithms designing for Mobile Robots (Swarm robots, Sensors, Unmanned aerial vehicles) and Wireless networks. However, I completed my graduation in mathematics from Scottish Church College, University of Calcutta and my M.Sc in Mathematics and Computing from IIT Guwahati but later found more interest in computer science and specifically in Distributed algorithms.
